Summer

Summer classes are structured similarly to our Public Classes. They will last 1-2 weeks (weekdays), 1 hour every day. They are open to rising grades 6th-11th, with some classes having more specific grade requirements.

Public and Private Classes/Tutoring are currently closed. Come back next fall to see what we'll be offering after summer!
