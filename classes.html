Classes

Summer

Summer classes are structured similarly to our Public Classes. They will last 1-2 weeks (weekdays), 1 hour every day. They are open to rising grades 5th-11th, with some classes having more specific grade requirements.

Public and Private Classes/Tutoring are currently closed. Come back next fall to see what we'll be offering after summer!

Registration forms for each class closes one week prior to the class beginning (ex. A class that starts June 9th will close registration June 2nd)

Intro to Game Design

Date(s): July 12th-July 23rd (weekdays only)

Time(s): 3pm to 4pm

Grade Range: 8th grade to 11th grade (rising)

Have you ever played games like Hollow Knight, Shovel Knight, or games not related to Knights and wondered how they were made? With this course, we’ll be discussing how to create a basic platformer using the open source game engine: Godot. You MUST know a programming language to take this class.

Registration has closed!

Website Design (HTML/CSS)

Date(s): August 2nd-August 13th (weekdays only)

Time(s): 2pm to 3pm

Grade Range: 5th grade to 9th grade (rising)

Interested in learning how to design a fully functioning website using HTML/CSS? This course is for you. Closely modeled after the website design course we offer during the school year, this course will teach your student the basics of Website Design.

Intro to Python

Date(s): July 5th-16th (weekdays only)

Time(s): 1pm to 2pm

Grade Range: 6th grade to 11th grade (rising)

This course will introduce Python, covering from printing “Hello World!” to writing basic programs. To those who took this course with us in the past, it will be essentially the same, but adapted for a daily two-week schedule rather than a weekly thirteen-week one.

Registration has closed!

Digital Art

Date(s): July 19th-July 30th (weekdays only)

Time(s): 1pm to 2pm

Grade Range: 5th grade to 8th grade (rising)

In this course, students will learn the basics of digital drawing softwares, including layers, blending, palette, and more! This is an introductory level course designed for students potentially interested in digital art. We will not be using drawing tablets or anything that students would be required to buy, but we will be open to helping students who would like to advance in digital art.

Registration has closed!

Java

Date(s): July 5th-July 16th (weekdays only)

Time(s): 2pm to 3pm

Grade Range: 8th grade to 11th grade (rising)

In the Java course, we'll start from the ground up by learning how to program and think logically. Then, using a project based learning style, we'll explore the basics of object oriented programming using the language.

Registration has closed!

Health Science

Date(s): August 2nd-August 13th (weekdays only)

Time(s): 10am to 11am

Grade Range: 7th grade to 10th grade (rising)

In the Health Science Course, we will be going over the structures and functions of each body system. Students will learn the diseases and symptoms associated with each body system as well as learning important medical terminology.
